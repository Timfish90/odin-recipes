Banana Bread

Description

Probaly one of the best things that can be turned into a loaf.

Ingredients

  • 2 Bananas
  • 1 egg
  • Borwn sugar
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Flour
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180C degrees.
  2. Mix Dry ingredients.
  3. Mash bananas with fork, place into separate bowl.
  4. Mix wet ingredients and stir into bananas.
  5. Mix wet and dry ingredients together.
  6. Place mixture into load tin
  7. Place in oven and cook for 20 minutes.
