Chicken

Description

A roast chicken can take time to prepare and cook but if cooked right, canturn out to be the best thing you've ever eaten.

Ingredients

  • One whole chicken
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Herbs and spices of choice

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180C degrees.
  2. Coat chicken in olive oil, salt and spices.
  3. Place chicken on cooking tray lined with baking sheet.
  4. Place chicken in oven and let roast for 45 minutes.
  5. Take chicken out and let cool for 5 minutes.
